About QCD Technology

QCD Technology (Singapore) Pte Ltd is part of the QCD Group, which has operations in Singapore, Malaysia and Indonesia.

QCD Technology (Singapore) Pte Ltd was established in August 1995 as an IT system and service provider.

With over 20 years of experience servicing IT systems and products, our company has worked with many of the leading brands in the industry including Apple, Compaq, Fujitsu, HP, etc.

Our Mission: To develop successful long-term relationships by providing high quality service to end customers and suppliers
